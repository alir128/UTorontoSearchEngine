FAQ Page

Q: How I can upload files?

Answer

First, you need to create or login with your own account. After you logged in, you will be able to upload files through the “Upload” option on the navigation bar.

Q: Why do I need to login?

Answer

If you logged in, you will have the ability to upload files, to see the files that are visible to your account other than just the public files.

Q: Is it possible that I can upload some private files that are only visible by myself?

Answer

Yes. That is doable and also very useful. You just need to upload the file just like you normally do. The only difference is that you need to select the access group as only visible to yourself when you are at the uploading page.

Q: I registered on the register page. Why am I still not able to login?

Answer

Great! You are only one step away. After filling all the information on the register page, you will receive an verification email on your registered email address. You just need to go to check your registered email and go to the link attached in the verification email. Then you are good to go.

Q: How can I join a group?

Answer

Joining to a group is easy and useful. You just need to go to your profile page. You will see the option “Enroll to a group”. But you will notice that, for the course groups and some other groups, you may need some permission to enroll into.

Q: Is it possible that I can create my own group?

Answer

Yes. It is absolutely possible for you to create your own group. But you need to know that you are not allowed to create any official course groups.

Q: What does tags do? Do I have to put some tags when I am uploading the file?

Answer

Tags are very good feature that can help others find your file more quickly. Tags are nothing but the keywords which can summarize or describe your file ideally. However, adding tags when uploading files are totally optional. It is not mandatory but we highly recommend you to put some ideal tags. That will not only benefit yourself, but also all the users of our searching engine.

Q: How can groups feature help me when I am using the search engine?

Answer

The groups feature is super useful. With this awesome feature, you no longer need to worry about the privacy of the files you uploaded. A simply step to fill the option of access group when you are uploading the file will protect your files so that they will only be visible by your assigned groups.
